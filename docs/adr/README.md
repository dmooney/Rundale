## ADR Template

When creating a new ADR, use the following sections:

### Status

Current status of the decision: Proposed, Accepted, Deprecated, or Superseded.

### Context

The problem or situation that motivates this decision. What forces are at play? What constraints exist?

### Decision

The change or approach that is being proposed or adopted. State the decision clearly and concisely.

### Consequences

The resulting effects of the decision, both positive and negative. What becomes easier? What becomes harder?

### Alternatives Considered

Other options that were evaluated and why they were not chosen.

### Related

Links to related documentation, other ADRs, or external references.
